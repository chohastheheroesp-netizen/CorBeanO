Eligibility Criteria

Our courses are designed for coffee enthusiasts at all levels. Review the general requirements below and use our checker tool to determine your eligibility for specific courses.

General Requirements

  • ✓ Minimum age: 18 years old
  • ✓ No formal coffee experience required for beginner courses
  • ✓ Able to commit to course schedule (typically 2-3 hours per session)
  • ✓ Genuine passion for coffee and willingness to learn

Course-Specific Prerequisites

  • Barista Fundamentals: None (open to all)
  • Advanced Brewing Techniques: Completion of Barista Fundamentals
  • Espresso Mastery: Completion of Barista Fundamentals
  • Coffee Cupping & Sensory: Completion of Advanced Brewing AND Espresso Mastery
