About Cor Bean O

Founded in 2012, Cor Bean O has grown from a 3-person startup to a 48-person team delivering world-class software solutions. We specialise in full-stack web development, mobile apps, and SaaS platforms. Our mission is simple: create exceptional digital experiences while mentoring the next generation of developers.

Every project is led by senior developers with 5+ years of experience. We're committed to not just building software, but cultivating a culture where junior developers learn, grow, and lead.
