About Cor Bean O

Founded in 2015, Cor Bean O is dedicated to elevating coffee culture through expert training and education. We partner with industry leaders and specialty coffee associations to deliver world-class courses that combine theory, technique, and practical application.

Our mission is to create a community of passionate coffee professionals who understand quality, sustainability, and the art of exceptional coffee. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned barista, we have a course designed for your journey.
